Direct Translation<\/summary>
\n

the best advice I got from language learning teachers, is to just think in Japanese instead of trying to do the translation in your head. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Because of this, I often do direct translations on the first pass of analyzing media.<\/p>\n<\/div><\/details><\/div>\n\n\n\n
